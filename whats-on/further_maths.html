Further Maths

Lecture Date: Thursday 24th of September

Time: 12:30pm - 6:30pm

Zoom codes will be sent out to your school emails.

There are multiple seminars for this subject

Further is the second most popular subject in the whole Victorian curriculum! Providing an overview of critical mathematics skills for future life. At BridgED, our further team have created a lecture for each module for you to revisit or learn the modules for your final exam!

Our tutors have developed comprehensive crash courses of each module, with specific focus on applying the content to a vast range of questions.

Key outcomes for the seminar are:

  • Techniques of answering questions to ensure achievement of marks
  • In depth understanding of research methods
  • Overview of whole study design curriculum
  • Exam tricks and tips!
