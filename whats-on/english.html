English

There are multiple seminars for this subject

English is THE most taken subject in VCE, with over 40,000 students taking the final exam every year. As a compulsory component of student’s studies, it serves as the most important subject. That’s why at BridgED we provide a range of resources for English students!

Our Seminars are:

  • Argument Analysis Seminar
  • Analytical Text Response Seminar
  • ​Comparative Text Response Seminars

Argument Analysis Seminar

Seminar Date:

A comprehensive overview of how students should analyse persuasive texts, covering structure and techniques, alongside tips and tricks to improve your analytical skills!

Key points covered in the seminar:

  • Exam tips for the argument analysis section of the exam
  • What to look out for when reading persuasive passages
  • Comprehensive overview of language techniques, and how to correctly analyse them!
  • Explanation of key mechanisms of argument analysis including argument structure, tone, audience and contentions
  • Structure, including how to write an introduction!

Text Response Seminar

Seminar Date:

Whether you haven’t read the book, or feel prepared and want to check your knowledge, this seminar will provide a detailed look into not only how to write an analytical text response, but also detailed explanation of themes and devices used in a range of texts.

Key points covered in the seminar:

  • What an analytical text response actually looks like
  • How to structure paragraphs and introductions!
  • Exam tips, and what the assessors are looking for!
  • In depth thematic insight into the texts seen below
