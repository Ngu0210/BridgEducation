Our Mission

BridgED is a non-profit organisation that is committed to providing students opportunities to explore the industries and fields that they are passionate about, and to make sure that they feel less restricted by their circumstances.

We want to empower and engage those students who come from a disadvantaged background, and help them in their pursuit of their personal goals. This begins with bridging the education opportunity gap seen within under-represented schools.

Our Story

At BridgED we believe that everyone deserves equal opportunities, in education and in life. Whether if you want to strive for a career as a journalist or an aerospace engineer, you should feel unrestrained, striving towards achieving your dream.

In Victoria there exists a dire gap in the opportunities presented to our students. That's why at BridgED, we're teaming up with past students and experienced tutors to help bridge that opportunity gap, to create a more equal and fair education system, and to empower every passionate student.
